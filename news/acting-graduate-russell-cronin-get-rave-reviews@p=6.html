Making Immersive Theatre

Immersive theatre is building momentum in the live performance industry. It is breaking down the norms of traditional theatre and creating a new atmosphere for the audience to get involved in and follow. After blowing up in major markets accross the UK and US, it is starting to find its way all the way to our shores here in Australia and naturally, some of our grads couldn't WAIT to sink their teeth into producing one.
