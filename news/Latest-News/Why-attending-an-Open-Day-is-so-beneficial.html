Why attending an Open Day is so beneficial

If you’re searching for information about a career in Film, Acting or Stage Management, then attending Open Days can be a critical part of the course- hunting experience. 

You’ll be able to soak up the culture, learn about the best way to finance your studies, sit in on a live stunt show, meet the tutors and speak to current students about their experiences. 

Top Benefits of attending AFTT’s September 17th Open Day

1.    Get all the information you need to kickstart your career at Australia’s most contemporary fully immersive arts academy.

2.    Check out all the facilities and equipment including our RED Cameras.
