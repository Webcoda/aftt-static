AFTT takes out the 2022 NSW Small Training Provider of the Year Award

AFTT announced as winner of Small Training Provider of the Year - NSW Training Awards 2022

The NSW Training Awards 2022 has announced AFTT as the winner of the Small Training Provider of the Year award for 2022!

A big thank you to all of our students, alumni and team for helping to make this happen!
