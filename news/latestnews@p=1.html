AFTT Partner with JMC Academy to Create First Ever National Acting Course

This February, AFTT were proud to partner with JMC Academy and bring our successful Acting course to JMC’s Melbourne campus! Now, we are happy to announce that the AFTT Acting course will be expanding to JMC’s Brisbane campus, creating the first national Acting program in Australia!
