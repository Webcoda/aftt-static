AFTT's High School Workshops are BACK These School Holidays

Are you in high school? Have a passion for Acting, Film or Theatre? Want to get ahead of the curve with the help of our amazing team? Then join us for AFTT's Free High School Workshops!

 

Here at AFTT, we are all about exploring your passion and providing our students the best possibility to be fearless in the pursuit of their dreams - and there's no better time to start exploring that during your school holidays!

If you are a high school student in grades 9 through to 12, we invite you to join us during the 7th - 9th of October 2020 for a FREE immersive workshop experience. Check out what workshops are available and hit that Register Button to join in on the fun.

ACTING TECHNIQUE - Creating a Character (Online Workshop)

Date & Time: Wednesday October 7th 2020, 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Learn all about acting approaches from the Morris technique to integrate instrument preparation into the creation of a character. Held over Zoom, this session will include:

  • Instrument approaches to connect to the inner life of a character
  • Developing impulse to work with acting freedom
  • Building given circumstances to create the world of a character
  • Performing a character organically on screen

Once registered, Zoom access links will be provided by email!

Register Now

THE FILM BREAKDOWN Online Workshop

Date & Time: Thursday October 8th 2020, 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Students will engage with one of AFTT’s experienced film trainers to simplify and breakdown the filmmaking process, from concept creation to delivery method. This workshop will be interactive and help develop and streamline your current or emerging process. Held over Zoom,  this session will include:

  • Ideas and concepts
  • Script Development (Structure/Character/Dialogue)

​Once registered, Zoom access links will be provided by email!

Register Now

THEATRE PRODUCTION On Campus Workshop

Date & Time: Friday October 9th 2020, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Location: Academy of Film, Theatre & Television, 41 Holt Street, Surry Hills, NSW, 2010

Join us in a unique experience to participate in a technical theatre & stage management workshop, where you will collaborate to help plan a performance from conception through to completion. This invaluable experience will give you the opportunity to get firsthand experience in professional stage management & technical theatre practices, by the end you will have what it takes to run a technical rehearsal and be an integral part of a theatre company. This session will be held on campus and will include:

  • Play the role of stage manager and director
  • Technical rehearsals
  • Collaborate in technical workshop
  • Show calling
  • Operating of lights and sound
