Fee Assistance for Domestic students

Students have the option of paying tuition fees prior to the commencement of each semester or deferring payment through FEE-HELP or VET Student Loan (VSL).

Unless applying for FEE-HELP or VSL, tuition fees are due TWO (2) weeks prior to the commencement of the course. Payment for all fees is in Australian dollars and payment can be made by cash, credit card or cheque. Please contact us for the account information.

Census Date The Census Date is the last day you can submit your Request for Commonwealth Assistance form (to access a Commonwealth supported place or FEE-HELP or VSL) or withdraw your enrolment without incurring a FEE-HELP or VSL debt.  If you do not submit your form by the Census Date, you will not be eligible to access a FEE-HELP or VSL loan for that semester. The Census Date is 20% of the way through a semester and is noted on the AFTT website or you can ask at Reception. More at http://studyassist.gov.au 
Note: The government applies a loan fee (25% of the tuition fees for HE and 20% of the tuition fees for VSL) to a  FEE-HELP or VSL loan. It does not count towards your total loan limit.

Deferment or Withdrawal

By completing a Request for Deferral or Withdrawal Form available from Reception, students may apply to defer their studies in the event of an unforeseen circumstance or illness for a maximum period of two semesters. Applications for deferment are accepted at AFTT’s discretion after a student interview. International students can only defer their enrolment on compassionate or compelling circumstances and deferring, suspending or cancelling enrolment may affect your Student Visa.
