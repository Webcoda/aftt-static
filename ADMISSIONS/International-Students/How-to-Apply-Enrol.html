How to Apply & Enrol

How to apply and enrol for International Students

Applying is easy! We will guide you through the application and enrolment process: 


Step 1: Apply

You can apply directly from our website – click here to apply now.

If you have a printed copy of the application form you can submit it via email to international@aftt.edu.au

You can also download the application form here.

Step 2: Submit documents 

You must submit the following documents:

  • Certified and translated (if originals are not in English) copy of school transcripts 
  • A copy of English language proficiency documents (see English language requirements for more information)
  • Evidence of successful completion of a senior Secondary School certificate or equivalent (transcripts and certificate must be translated and certified). 
  • Certified copy of your passport
  • Certified copy of current visa, if currently in Australia
  • You can attach these documents to your online application, email them to us international@aftt.edu.au or post to 41 Holt Street, Surry Hills, NSW, 2010, Australia.

For information on how to certify a document please click here.

Step 3: Interview

We will contact you to arrange an interview, either in person or via video call.

Step 4: Audition (for acting students only)

Acting applicants are required to attend an audition to demonstrate their ability. Students applying from overseas may film the audition and upload to a video streaming site (set to private). Please submit the relevant links to the Admissions Officer. 

Step 5: Formal offer (Letter of Offer)

Applicants will be notified of the interview / audition outcome and successful applicants will be issued a Letter of Offer. The Letter of Offer will be accompanied by an invoice for the first term’s tuition fees and the enrolment fee.

Step 6: Enrolment process 

To accept an offer you must:
•    Complete and sign the Enrolment Form
•    Complete and sign the Enrolment Acceptance Agreement
•    Pay enrolment fee of AUD $150.00
•    Pay first semester tuition fee
•    Organise Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC). A quote for health cover will appear on your invoice. Health cover may be purchased through AFTT or you can arrange your own. 

STEP 7: Apply for your visa 

AFTT will issue an Electronic Confirmation of Enrolment (eCoE) so that you can apply for a study visa.

STEP 8: Start your creative future at AFTT!
