2016 Student Film Screening Evening

AFTT is proud to present the annual 2016 Student Film Screening night. Join us in celebrating the hard work of our students this year. 

This event will showcase the best student films of 2016. We invite you to come along with your family and/or friends to watch these films on the big screen and to celebrate the hard work, and creativity of our talented students.

Thursday 24th November, 2016
